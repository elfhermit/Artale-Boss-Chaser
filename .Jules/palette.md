## 2024-05-18 - Missing ARIA Labels and Unlinked Form Controls
**Learning:** This application relies heavily on icon-only buttons (like `+`/`-` channel controls and delete buttons) and uses custom styled inputs. Many of these icon-only buttons lack `aria-label` attributes, making them inaccessible to screen readers. Furthermore, `<label>` elements for inputs (like channel and notes) are often not explicitly linked using `for` attributes, breaking form control context.
**Action:** When adding new interactive elements or form controls, always ensure icon-only buttons have descriptive `aria-label` attributes and that `<label>` elements are correctly associated with their corresponding inputs using `for` matching the input's `id`. Decorative icons within buttons should also have `aria-hidden="true"`.
